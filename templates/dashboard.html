BAPATLA ENGINEERING COLLEGE

This College is one of the few Engineering Colleges in Andhra Pradesh, which is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The Five B. Tech. Courses which were accredited thrice are: Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electonics and Communications Engineering, Electrical and Electonics Engineering & Mechanical Engineering. The Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering and Chemical Engineering courses were accredited twice and Infomation Technology was also accredited once. The College became AUTONOMOUS in the academic Year 2010 - 11.

CHIRALA ENGINEERING COLLEGE

The Chirala Engineering College was established in academic year 2001 by GSR & TSR Educational Society with a clear objective of providing quality technical education in tune with international standards and contemporary global requirements. The college is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), affiliated to the JNTU Kakinada and recognised as the Jawahar Knowledge Centre (JKC) by the government of A.P. It is the first engineering college in Prakasam district to attain ISO:9001:2000 certification.

VRS & YRN COLLEGE

Vutukuri Ramayya Sreshti & Yarlagadda Ranganayakulu Naidu College, Chirala, was started as VRS College in 1951 with the munificence of Sri Vutukuri Ramayya Sreshti, to cater to the educational needs of backward and deprived rural sections of the then Guntur District of Madras State. VRS College has been rechristened as VRS & YRN College since 1962, after the donation of an eminent philanthropist Sri Yarlagadda Ranganayakulu Naidu through his charitable trust for the development of the College.
